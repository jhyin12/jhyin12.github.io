Jianhua Yin (尹建华)

I'm Jianhua Yin, a tenure-track Associate Professor of Prof. Liqiang Nie's team in School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University (Qingdao Campus). Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in 2017, advised by Prof. Jianyong Wang (IEEE Fellow). In 2012, I received my bachelor's degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University.

My research interests include Data Mining and Machine Learning.
